About Me

I am an accomplished media and communication scholar with over 15 years of academic experience. My expertise spans public relations, advertising, media translation, and digital communication strategies. I have authored 11 published books and actively conduct research in media studies with a focus on social media impact and future trends in communication.

Throughout my career at Tanta University, I have dedicated myself to advancing the field of media studies through innovative teaching methods, comprehensive research, and practical applications of communication theories. My work bridges traditional media concepts with modern digital platforms, preparing students for the evolving media landscape.
